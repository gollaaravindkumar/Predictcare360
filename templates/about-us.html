About Us

PredictCare360 is an AI-powered health platform that provides comprehensive risk assessment for multiple major diseases, including heart disease, diabetes, liver disease, kidney disease, and breast cancer. It enables users to receive instant personalized health insights, track and monitor their medical data, and get recommendations for lifestyle, nutrition, and treatment. Patients can also access information about hospitals and doctors, and conveniently schedule appointments online.
